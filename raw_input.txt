Paragraph 1What time is it? That's a very good question! From morning until night, clocks help us to plan our day. They tell us when things begin and when they end. Before clocks, people told time by watching the sun move across the sky. They started work when the sun came up in the east. At noon, when the sun was very high, they ate and rested. When the sun disappeared in the west, they went to bed. 3【中文翻譯】現在幾點？這是一個非常好的問題！從早到晚，時鐘幫助我們計畫我們的一天 3。它們告訴我們事情何時開始，何時結束 3。在有時鐘之前，人們透過觀察太陽在天空中的移動來辨別時間 3。當太陽從東方升起時，他們開始工作 3。在正午，當太陽非常高的時候，他們吃飯並休息 3。當太陽在西方消失時，他們就去睡覺 3。
Paragraph 2Today, we know that the sun doesn't move around the Earth. The Earth turns, or spins, on an imaginary line called an axis. As the Earth turns, the sun seems to be moving across the sky. The time it takes for the Earth to make one complete turn is called a day. A day is divided into hours, minutes, and seconds. There are 24 hours in a day. There are 60 minutes in an hour. How many minutes are there in a day? Multiply 24 hours times 60 minutes to get the answer. 24 X 60 = 1,440. There are 1,440 minutes in one day! 4【中文翻譯】今天，我們知道太陽並沒有繞著地球轉 4。地球繞著一條叫做軸的假想線轉動，或者說自轉 4。隨著地球轉動，太陽似乎在天空中移動 4。地球完成一次完整自轉所需的時間稱為一天 4。一天被劃分為小時、分鐘和秒 4。一天有24小時 4。一小時有60分鐘 4。一天有多少分鐘呢？將24小時乘以60分鐘就能得到答案 4。24 x 60 = 1,440 4。一天有1,440分鐘！ 4
Paragraph 3We measure time by other movements, too. The Earth travels around, or orbits, the sun. The time it takes for the Earth to go around the sun once is called a year. One year is equal to 12 months, or just over 52 weeks. To be very accurate, it takes the Earth 365 days, 5 hours, 49 minutes, and 12 seconds to go around the sun. 5【中文翻譯】我們也透過其他的運動來測量時間 5。地球繞著太陽運行，或是說公轉 5。地球繞太陽一圈所需的時間稱為一年 5。一年等於12個月，或是剛好超過52週 5。非常精確地說，地球繞太陽一圈需要365天5小時49分又12秒 5。
Paragraph 4We divide the Earth into 24 time zones. Each time zone is one hour apart from the next time zone. For example, when it is 10:00 a.m. where you live, it is 10:00 p.m. on the opposite side of the world. In two hours, it will be noon where you live, but on the other side of the world, it will be 12:00 at night! Thanks to clocks and calendars, we can measure time very easily. However, it is the movement of the Earth that really allows us to measure time. 6【中文翻譯】我們將地球劃分為24個時區 6。每個時區與下一個時區相差一小時 6。舉例來說，當你住的地方是上午10:00時，在地球的另一端就是晚上10:00 6。兩個小時後，你住的地方會是正午，但在地球的另一端，將會是半夜12:00！ 6 幸虧有時鐘和日曆，我們可以非常輕鬆地測量時間 6。然而，真正讓我們能夠測量時間的，是地球的運動 6。

文法 1: Future Facts with Will (使用 Will 表達未來的客觀事實)
English Explanation: Use will to talk about future facts. Remember, a fact is something that is true. 7
Chinese Explanation: 我們使用 will (將會) 來談論「未來一定會發生的客觀事實」（不是個人計畫或猜測，而是大自然規律或不可改變的狀態） 7。
Example Sentences:
In two hours, it will be noon where you live. (兩個小時後，你住的地方將會是正午。) 6
On the other side of the world, it won't be noon. (在世界的另一端，將不會是正午。) 6
It will be 12:00 at night! (將會是半夜 12 點！) 6
You will be 8 years old tomorrow. (你明天就會滿 8 歲了。) 10
The sun will rise tomorrow at 5:00 a.m. (明天早上 5 點太陽將會升起。) 11
文法 2: Definite Article "The" with Countries (特定國家名稱前面加上定冠詞 The)
English Explanation: We use the before a country name when the country name is plural (like the United States), or when it has the word Kingdom or Republic. 8
Chinese Explanation: 通常國家名字前面不加 the。但是，如果國家名字是「複數形態」（例如 United States），或是名稱中包含 Kingdom (王國)、Republic (共和國)，前面就必須加上定冠詞 the 8。
Example Sentences:
He lives in the United States. (他住在美國。) 9
She lives in France. (她住在法國。法國為一般名詞，不加 the) 9
We live in the United Kingdom. (我們住在英國。) 9
We traveled to the Republic of Ireland. (我們去愛爾蘭共和國旅行。) 9
Our class is studying about the United Arab Emirates. (我們班正在學習關於阿拉伯聯合大公國的知識。) 9
